## Audit Lesson: grep false positive on author field

When checking author coverage with line-level grep for `author:`, the pattern also matches prose containing the word "author" (e.g., "authorization", "authoring") at matching indent. This caused google-workspace-auth to appear as having an author when it didn't. Fix: verify matches fall between `---` delimiters (YAML frontmatter context), or use a YAML parser instead of line-level grep.
